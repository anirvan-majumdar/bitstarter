The world of digital communication has been horribly compromised. Edward Snowden's revalations have confirmed what we believed for quite some time. It is the extent that we had no clue of. Every single platform of communication, seems to have been compromised.

Khoofiya  holds the key to a secured platform of communication!

But even the giants of this industry couldn't hold fast against the might of governmental agencies, what chance does Khoofiya hold?

The secret lies in not storing anything!

  • All your messages will be encrypted using asymmetric keys.
  • None of your messages will be stored on our servers.
  • Every piece of multimedia will be immediately transferred to your recipients.


In short, if there's no data to be snooped upon, who's going to steal it anyways?

We just wanted to have a way to share our things privately amongst our friends and family. And then we thought, why not just build it for everyone out there?!

FAQ

What does Khoofiya mean?
It's a Hindi word, which translates to "Secret" in English.
If you're not going to store user info, how will you make money?
Khoofiya will primarily rely on the funds we can raise through this campaign, and an annual fee, which shouldn't be too high to deter users. In time, we would explore other avenues of revenue based on sponsored messages, something like Twitter does. But obviously, the ads won't be targeted, since we really won't have any relevant user data.
What about my contacts list? Won't you be storing that?
That will be entirely your decision. If you want portability of list across devices, then yes, you'll need to explicitly give us permission to store your contacts. Otherwise, we will simply rely on Global and encrypted user ID hashes to match up your contacts.
